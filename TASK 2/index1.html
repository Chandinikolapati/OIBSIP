Kalpana Chawla

india born-American Astronaut

Here's a time line of Kalpana's life

Early life
  • 17 Mar 1962: kalpana Chawla was born in Karnal in the state of Haryana to Banarasi Lal Chawla and Sanjyothi Chawla.
  • 1978: She completed her earlier schooling at Tagore Baal Niketan Sr,Sec.Scjool, Karnal.
  • 1982: She completed Bachelor of Engineering degree in Aeronautical Engineering at Punjab Engineering College Enginnering College at Chandigrah i 1982
  • 1983: on 2 Dec, Kalpana Chawla was married to Jean-Pierre harrison at the age of 21.
  • 1984: She moved to the United Statesand obtained a M.S. degree in aerospace engineering from the university of Texas at Arlington.
  • 1986: Kalpana Chawla went on to earn a second M.S. degree and a PhD in aerospace engineering in 1988 from the University of Colorado at Boudler.
Career
  • 1988: Chawla began working at NASA Ames Research Center, where she did computational fluid dynamics(CFD) research on vertical and/or short take-off and landing(V/STOL)concepts.
  • 1993: she joined Overset methods, inc. as vice president and Research Scientist Specializing in simulation of moving multiple body problems.
  • 1997: she joined the corps in march and was selected for her first flight in 1997.
  • 2002: Chawla was selected for her second flight as part of the crew of STS-107 which was repeatedly delayed due to scheduling conflicts and technical problems such as the July 2002 discovery of cracks in the shuttle engine flow liners.
  • 2003: On 16 January, Chawla finally returned to space aboard Spave Shuttle Columbia on the ill-fated STS-107 mission. During the launch, a piece of foam insulation broke off from the shuttle external tank and struck the port wing of the orbiter.on 1st feb she died in the disaster, along with the other 6 six crew members😑.
